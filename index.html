About Us

The Green Mirage is a solo-executed, NYUAD-ready youth project that blends environmental research, business ethics, and economics. Built on IB frameworks and UN SDG values, we investigate whether companies live up to their eco-branding.

Audit Reports

We evaluate Indian and global brands using the Green Mirage Index (GMI), rating transparency, environmental claims, and ethical practices.

  • Taj Hotels: 81/100 — Responsible
  • Blue Tokai: 67/100 — Moderate
  • ITC: 48/100 — Greenwashed
